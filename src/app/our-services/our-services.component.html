Pocket Money

In return for their help, au pairs receive pocket money from their host families on a weekly basis, Au pairs are entitled to the minimum wage in United states. The current minimum wage after tax per month is a $3,700USD monthly and $300USD per week.

Board and Loding

During their entire stay, your au pairs lives in you home and are entitled to a private furnished bedroom and free access to meals. This also applies in case of illness and during the au pair's holidays.
